1. Sign in and Sign up

1) Sign in

2) Sign up

  • Go to site

    Go to https://histutor.lifove.net/ and sign in using handong email.

    If you try to sign in using another email, it will not work properly, so make sure to sign in using the Handong email.

  • Check and edit student number and name

Make sure that your student number and name is correct; otherwise, edit it in the format of student number + name.

Only use the two first numbers of the student number.

예) 217***** Jongeun Kim → 17Jongeun Kim

  • Enter your phone number

Enter your phone number, and the sign up process is done.

2. Attend tutoring sessions

You can attend to tutor sessions or coding hour tutoring created by a tutor.

1) Check tutoring list

On the main page, press the title of the tutoring that you want to attend from the list of tutorings.

2) Waiting room

Histutor's tutoring is carried out in 1-on-1.

Wait until you are called out by the tutor.

You can check the total number of queues (number of tutees waiting), your queueing order, and how the number of tutees waiting in front and behind you.

When it's your turn, the screen automatically moves to the tutoring room with a notification that your turn has come.

❗Warning❗ Please note that if you leave or refresh the waiting room page, your waiting order will be pushed back, so please be aware of this.

3) Attend tutoring

Now you get to start the tutoring with the tutor! 🥳

If you need help or have any questions, please chat about it. 🙌

When your turn is over after asking all the questions, you will be exited to the main page by the tutor.

3. Tutoring Recruitment

1) Create tutoring room

Create a room to ask tutors for help of your need.

  • Create tutoring room

    Press the Creating a tutoring room from the top right of the page.

  • Enter the title

    Enter the subject as detailed as possible so that the tutor can know in what part you need help.

    예) I need help in accessing my root account after installing MySQL.

  • Completed creating tutoring room

Now the tutoring room is created!

The room is created and the room status is set to Recruiting Tutor.

2) Start tutoring

You can talk with the tutor about the problem you are facing and receive help.

If the tutor decides that he/she can help you with the problem, the tutor will assign him/herself as the tutor of the tutoring room and the status of the room will change to Tutoring in Progress.

Once the problem is solved, the tutor ends the tutoring.

3) Write a review (optional)

Leave a review of the tutoring.

Feel free to fill out the contents.

A simple guide line of the content includes on what you have received help, how the tutor helped you, and how it actually helped you.

4. MyPage

On My Page, you can see a list of the tutor sessions that you have recruited or have participated in.

5. Reply to comments function 🤩

We introduce you the replying to comments function of Histutor.

1) Double click the message

Double-click✨ the message that you want to reply, and the comment window will show.

2) Write the reply to the comment

You can check the pretty comments you have made after writing and sending the reply contents of the message.
